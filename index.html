Welcome to Adventure Rafting, the home of rafting. Adventure Rafting is an international company based in fifty-one unique locations ranging from bottom of the world New Zealand to top of the world Greenland. We have been operating for over twenty-five years and have been the host to a 100,000 amazing raft trips.

Scenic Rafting

Mesmerizing safe rafting fun for the whole family. Come join us for tranquil immersion in the many stunning abnormalities of nature. Choose from one of our three unique scenic adventures. Family Rafting a 3-5 hour fun scenic trip sightseeing down the innumerable stunning rivers of the world. Exploration Rafting a 4-7 hour adventure down beautiful rivers, with the main focal point being exploring the rare locations that surround the river. Romantic rafting - under the cover of darkness, illuminated by traditional japanese lamps, a perfect unforgettable date, honeymoon or even proposal.

Adrenaline Rafting

Extremely exhilarating, intense rafting fun for ages 16 and up. Come join us for 2-4 hours of blood-pumping action as our experienced guides blast you down the most dangerous of rivers. Choose from grade 3 rapids for intermediate rafting fun, or grade 4 rapids for advanced challenge or, for the inner thrillseeker, grade 5 rapids for the most intense rafting trip of your life.

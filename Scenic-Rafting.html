Mesmerizing safe rafting fun for the whole family. Come join us for tranquil immersion in the many stunning abnormalities of nature. Choose from one of our three unique scenic adventures. Family Rafting a 3-5 hour fun scenic trip sightseeing down the innumerable stunning rivers of the world. Exploration Rafting a 4-7 hour adventure down beautiful rivers, with the main focal point being exploring the rare locations that surround the river. Romantic rafting - under the cover of darkness, illuminated by traditional japanese lamps, a perfect unforgettable date, honeymoon or even proposal.

Family Rafting

Come bring the whole family along for safe rafting fun (Ages 8+). The family rafting trip takes between 3-5 hours (depending on the location) and consists of a guided scenic rafting trip down beautiful rivers that ends with a picnic lunch. All rafting gear is supplied and prices may vary between locations. Contact your local branch to find out more.

“I highly recommend ‘Family Rafting’ to anyone that likes bumpy rides, scenic views and picnics by the river. So cool.”

Exploration Rafting

Great for group adventures Exploration Rafting is safe rafting fun for ages 12 and up. The Exploration Rafting trip takes between 3-6 hours (depending on location) and consists of a normal rafting trip with numerous stops in order to explore the beautiful area surrounding the river. At one of these stops, lunch will be provided . All rafting gear is supplied and prices may vary between locations. Contact your local branch to find out more.

Romantic Rafting

Romantic Rafting is almost completely customisable to suit your needs. The Romantic Rafting trip usually takes around 2 hours and generally consists of a midnight, moonlit trip guided by traditional Japanese floating lamps down a gentle river. Call to organise and tailor the romantic rafting trip to enhance the moment. All rafting gear is supplied and prices may vary between locations. Contact your local branch to find out more.
